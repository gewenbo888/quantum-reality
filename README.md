# The Nature of Quantum Reality · 量子的本质

A monumental, cinematic, bilingual (EN · 中文) cathedral of quantum understanding. A scroll-driven journey from Planck's first quantum to modern quantum computing — through wave–particle duality, superposition, entanglement, the observer effect, decoherence and the deepest interpretations of physics — connecting quantum mechanics to consciousness, information, cosmology, simulation theory and the philosophy of existence itself.

Every section, label, equation and interactive element switches instantly between English and Chinese through an animated quantum language toggle. The **Quantum Lab** runs real experiments live in the browser: a single-particle double-slit, an interactive Bloch sphere with real gates, an entangled Bell pair, a two-qubit circuit playground and a quantum random-number generator — every amplitude and probability computed in real time.
